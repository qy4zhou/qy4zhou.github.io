Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS)

I am working on a team to develop a Computerized Maintenance Management System for Conestoga College. It involves 18 schools across 4 campuses and 1200 machines. The system will be used to track the schools' machine-related inventory, work requests, maintenance schedule, and reports. This project is being developed in an agile environment, on a Microsoft platform (ie MS SQL and Visual Studio).
